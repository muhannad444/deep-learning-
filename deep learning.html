📖 How This System Works

1️⃣ Define Your Goal

Clearly state WHAT you want to achieve and WHY it matters. This gives your brain purpose and focus.

📋 The Deep Understanding Prompt

Explain this topic simply:
[ write your topic here ]

Focus on:
• Why does it exist?
• Why should we care?
• What is its purpose and benefit?
• How does it work? (step by step)
• What are the main parts?
• Give an example
• Give an category
• What will I gain after learning it?
• How does it help in school and real life?
• What are the 20% core concepts that generate 80% of understanding?
• What information can I give to someone struggling with this topic?
Use very simple words (like teaching a 7-year-old).
